<section>
  <h2>Phobia Log (Session Summary)</h2>
  <p>This tool summarises a single exposure attempt, pairing time spent with how anxiety shifted.</p>
  <p class="notice">Purpose: keep a brief record of what occurred without inferring progress or regression.</p>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>What it is for</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Noting exposure type, duration, and feelings at the start and end.</li>
    <li>Capturing notable thoughts that arose.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>What it is not for</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Measuring success or failure.</li>
    <li>Creating trends, streaks, or comparisons across sessions.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
