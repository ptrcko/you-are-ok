<section>
  <h2>Exposure Log</h2>
  <p>This tool records observations during an exposure session, focusing on what happened rather than outcomes.</p>
  <p class="notice">Purpose: note times, sensations, and thoughts without expecting anxiety to change.</p>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>What it is for</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Capturing details from a single exposure session.</li>
    <li>Optionally linking back to a fear hierarchy you created.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>What it is not for</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Judging whether exposure was successful.</li>
    <li>Creating streaks, reminders, or targets.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
