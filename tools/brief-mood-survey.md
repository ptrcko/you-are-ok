<section>
  <h2>Brief Mood Survey</h2>
  <p>This tool offers a simple snapshot of current emotional states across a few dimensions.</p>
  <p class="notice">Purpose: track feelings in the moment without interpretation, scoring, or comparison.</p>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>What it is for</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Noting how you feel right now across several feelings.</li>
    <li>Keeping each check-in independent of the others.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>What it is not for</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Diagnosing or predicting anything about you.</li>
    <li>Triggering alerts, goals, or advice.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
