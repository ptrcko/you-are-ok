<section>
  <h2>Relapse Awareness Log</h2>
  <p>This tool acknowledges setbacks as expected and invites a calm record of what happened.</p>
  <p class="notice">Purpose: note events, emotions, and helpful responses without comparison to a baseline.</p>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>What it is for</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Capturing an upsetting event and the emotions before and after.</li>
    <li>Collecting thoughts and responses you found useful.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>What it is not for</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Scoring relapse severity or frequency.</li>
    <li>Judging whether a response was correct or sufficient.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
