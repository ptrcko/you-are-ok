<section>
  <h2>Cost–Benefit Analysis</h2>
  <p>This tool collects advantages and disadvantages of a belief or behaviour so you can view them together.</p>
  <p class="notice">Purpose: list observations without weighting, scoring, or nudging toward a decision.</p>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>What it is for</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Setting down pros and cons in one place.</li>
    <li>Allowing contradictory points to coexist.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>What it is not for</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Calculating totals or recommending choices.</li>
    <li>Judging which points matter most.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
